Vegetarian High-Protein Weekly Menu

Day 1

Breakfast

  • Protein shake (1.5 scoops plant protein + almond milk + banana)
  • Oats + chia seeds + almonds

Lunch

  • Quinoa (1.5 cups cooked)
  • Lentil curry (1.5 cups cooked lentils)
  • Mixed veggies

Dinner

  • Tofu stir-fry (150g tofu)
  • Brown rice (1 cup cooked)
  • Steamed broccoli

Day 2

Breakfast

  • Greek yogurt (200g) + mixed berries + walnuts
  • Protein shake (1 scoop plant protein + almond milk)

Lunch

  • Chickpea and spinach stew (1.5 cups cooked chickpeas)
  • Bulgur wheat (1.5 cups cooked)

Dinner

  • Tempeh sauté (150g) with bell peppers
  • Quinoa (1 cup cooked)
  • Roasted asparagus

Day 3

Breakfast

Dinner

  • Paneer tikka (150g paneer)
  • Couscous (1 cup cooked)
  • Steamed green beans
